how to replace a bike inner tube

Remove bike tire:

    1)Deflate inner tube
    2)Insert pry bar into the lining of the tire and bar tire out from the rims.
    3)Insert second pry bar into tire and pry tir over the spokes.
    4)pull pry bar around the tire causing the tire to derail.
    5)Remove tire.

Insert New inner tube:

    1)Put a little bit of air in inner tube.
    2)put inner tube around the rim.
    3)put valve through the hole in the rim.

Put tire back on:

    1)slide tire around innertube.
    2)tuck inner tube into the rim.
    3)Inflate inner tube.
